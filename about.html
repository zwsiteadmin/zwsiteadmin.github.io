Have a clear vision of what you want to achieve and set specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART) goals. Whether it's career advancement, personal growth, or financial planning, outline your desired outcomes and break them into smaller, manageable steps.

Understand the internal and external factors that can affect your goals. This includes strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats (SWOT analysis).
